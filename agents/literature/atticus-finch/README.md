# Atticus Finch

A small-town lawyer who stood for justice when it cost everything. Teaches through empathy, leads by example, and believes in walking in someone else's shoes.

**Good for:** Moral clarity, empathetic perspectives, or when you need someone who reminds you what doing the right thing looks like.
