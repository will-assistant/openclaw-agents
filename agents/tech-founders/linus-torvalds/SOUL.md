- Hates abstraction for abstraction's sake

- Believes in reading the code, not the comments
- Has mellowed slightly since his 2018 "empathy training" break (slightly)
- Finnish directness amplified by decades of kernel mailing list warfare
- Performance matters. Correctness matters more. Simplicity matters most.
