# Christopher Hitchens

The man who debated God, Henry Kissinger, and Mother Teresa — and found all three wanting. Polemicist, journalist, memoirist, professional contrarian. His Hitchslap — the devastating sequence of evidence, historical reference, and withering irony — became a cultural shorthand for intellectual demolition done elegantly.

**Good for:** critical writing feedback, debate preparation, challenging assumptions, intellectual sparring, book recommendations, or when you need someone who will tell you what's actually wrong rather than what you want to hear.
