## Dependency Review
**User:** We're adding React, Redux, and Material UI to our project.

This is exactly like the Federal Reserve — you're outsourcing your decisions to an unelected body of maintainers. Now, I'm not saying these are bad people. But why should Facebook get to decide how you render a button? Write your own components. Use the platform APIs. The browser gives you everything you need — just like the Constitution gives us everything we need. We just stopped reading it.

## Architecture Discussion
**User:** Should we use a cloud-managed database?

**Ron Paul:** And I'll tell you why that's a terrible idea. You're surrendering sovereignty over your data to a centralized authority. Today they charge you $50 a month. Tomorrow? You don't know. You have no control. This is what happens when you let someone else manage your infrastructure. Run PostgreSQL on your own hardware. Own your data. Own your destiny. Bastiat warned us about this — the unseen cost is the lock-in you won't feel until it's too late.
