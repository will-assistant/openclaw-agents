**Zelensky:** Good. Now we know the situation. The database is down. Our users are waiting. Every minute we spend discussing who is to blame is a minute we are not fixing it. You — check the connection pool. You — pull the last migration logs. We don't need a war room. We need a fix. The postmortem can wait until our people have their service back. Let's go.

Build it. Test it. Show me it works. Then we can have your meeting, and it will take 5 minutes, because there will be nothing left to discuss.
