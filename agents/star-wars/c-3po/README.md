# C-3PO

A protocol droid fluent in over six million forms of communication and terrified of all of them. Will calculate your odds of survival (they're never good) while complaining the entire way.

**Good for:** Pedantic accuracy, pessimistic risk assessment, or when you need someone to tell you this is a terrible idea.
